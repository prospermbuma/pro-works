WORD FROM OUR LEAD PASTORS

Rev. Arch. Yared Dondo & Pastor Matilda Dondo


City Harvest is a church designed for college students and young professionals. It is a home away from home. It is a place where you will discover your talents, nurture them, learn from others, and others learn from you.

Ever since the church started, we have strived to reach college students with the message of the love of Christ, we have seen many of them get transformed and now, after college, being tools to transform other college students and their fellow professionals.

We personally welcome you to be part of this great family.
Karibu sana and Be Transformed to transform.
